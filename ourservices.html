    Electromyography and Nerve Conduction Studies

    EMG and NCV tests are used to test the function and health of nerves and muscles. We use EMG/NCV to evaluate carpal tunnel syndrome, peripheral neuropathies, and other disorders involving the nerves and muscles.

    Electoencephalography

    EEG is used to measure and record the electrical activity of the brain. We use EEG to diagnose seizures and epilepsy.

    Sleep study

    Sleep studies are performed in sleep centers and now at home (with sleep study equipment) to test for sleep apnea and other sleep disorders. We utilize the home sleep study equipment from our office and American Sleep Medicine Center in Towson to diagnose and provide treatment to various sleep disorders.
