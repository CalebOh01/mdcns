    Sangjin Oh, MD

    Board certified in Neurology and Psychiatry, Sleep Medicine, and Vascular Neurology

    Dr. Sangjin Oh is a Board Certified neurologist with a speciality in Multiple Sclerosis. He has extensive training in all neurological diseases of adults and children. Dr. Oh received his Bachelors of Arts in neuroscience from the University of California at Los Angeles. He completed his medical degree at St. Louis University. He went on to complete his neurology residency at the University of Maryland Medical Center. During his residency, he served as a chief resident and also received the prestigious Humanism Honors Society Award. He continued his training at the University of Maryland with a fellowship in Multiple Sclerosis and Immunology. What’s Up Annapolis Magazine recently selected Dr. Oh as one of the Top Docs in the Anne Arundel County area. The National MS Society named Dr. Oh the Healthcare Professional of the Year in 2014.

    Robert Reif, MD

    Board certified in Neurology and Psychiatry

    Dr. Robert Reif is a Board Certified neurologist with particular interest in neuromuscular disease, headache, and stroke. He received his Bachelor of Arts degree in biology and chemistry at St. Mary’s College of Maryland and his medical degree at the University of Maryland School of Medicine. He completed his residency at University of Maryland Medical Center, where he served as Chief Resident of Education and was elected to the Alpha Omega Alpha Honor Society. He remained at University of Maryland to complete a fellowship in neurophysiology with focus on neuromuscular disease. He subsequently joined WellSpan Neurology in Pennsylvania where he earned numerous teaching awards from the Drexel School of Medicine and Penn State College of Medicine, where he was a clinical assistant professor. He has recently moved back to Maryland to bring his expertise in all areas of adult neurology back home.
